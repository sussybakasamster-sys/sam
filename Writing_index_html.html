FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do you offer same-day service?
Yes! In most cases we can dispatch a technician the same day you call, often within 2 hours. For non-urgent work, we offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Absolutely. All ProServ Elite technicians hold the required state licenses for their trade, carry full liability insurance, and pass background checks before joining our team.
How does flat-rate pricing work?
Before any work begins, we provide a written quote based on the job — not the clock. You approve the price upfront, so there are zero surprises on your bill. We never charge by the hour.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all of Los Angeles County including Los Angeles, Santa Monica, West Hollywood, Burbank, Pasadena, Glendale, Long Beach, Torrance, Culver City, Inglewood, and many more.
Do you offer emergency services?
Yes, our emergency line is available 24/7, 365 days a year. Whether it's a burst pipe at midnight or a power outage on a holiday, call (310) 555-0199 and we'll dispatch immediately.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, check, and Zelle. For larger projects, we also offer flexible financing options with approved credit.
